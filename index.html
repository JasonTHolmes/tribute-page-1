Muhammad Ibn Abdullah

Prophet of Islam

  • April 22nd, 571AD - The Prophet of Islam is Born in Mecca in modern day Saudi Arabia
  • 610AD - First received revelation delivered to Prophet Muhammad from Angel Gabriel
  • June 622 - The Prophet and his followers make their first migration from Mecca to Medinah
  • March 13th, 624AD - The Battle Of Badr takes place resulting in the Muslim's first victory
  • January 11th, 630AD - The conquest of Mecca - the end of wars between the Muslims and the Quraysh
  • June 8th, 632AD - The Prophet Of Islam dies after falling severely ill.
“The best among you are those who have the best manners and character.” Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)
